question: What is a neurodevelopmental assessment?
answer: "A neurodevelopmental assessment is a comprehensive evaluation process
  that examines how your brain processes information, manages social
  communication, and responds to various situations. It involves clinical
  interviews asking about your functioning throughout your whole life time,
  standardised questionnaires including the Adolescent/Adult Sensory Profile
  (AASP), and gold-standard standardised assessments including the Autism
  Diagnostic Observation Schedule (ADOS) to identify and diagnose conditions
  like autism. "
