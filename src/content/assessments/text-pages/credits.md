The bespoke fonts used in the logo and titles throughout this website was designed by Adriana for the sole use of Together Assessments. It is based off of principals publicied by Sylexiad, OpenDyslexic and Hyperlegible. While not a font option on this website, a thanks is extended to the Braille Institute for developing and generously sharing the hyperlegible font. <https://www.brailleinstitute.org/freefont/>
