question: What is a neurodevelopmental assessment?
answer: A neurodevelopmental assessment is a comprehensive evaluation process
  that examines how your brain processes information, manages attention, and
  responds to various situations. It involves clinical interviews, standardised
  questionnaires, and observational assessments to identify conditions like ADHD
  or autism.
